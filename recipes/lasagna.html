Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese light
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic,onion,and Italian seasoning

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Boil the lasagna noodles until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, cook ground beef with garlic and onion until browned. Drain the fat.
  4. Stir in tomato sauce and Italian seasoning. Simmer for about 10 minutes.
  5. In a separate bowl, mix ricotta cheese with one egg and a bit of salt and pepper.
  6. In a baking dish, layer noodles, sauce, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella. Repeat layers until full.
  7. Top with mozzarella and parmesan cheese.
  8. Bake for 25–30 minutes or until bubbly and golden on top.
  9. Let it rest for 10 minutes before serving.

Note: This is my favourite lasagna recipe
