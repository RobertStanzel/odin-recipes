Fried chicken recipe

Ingredients

  • Chicken pieces (legs, thighs, or breasts)
  • Flour
  • Salt and pepper
  • Buttermilk
  • Oil for frying
  • Spices (paprika, garlic powder, onion powder)

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken in buttermilk and spices for a few hours.
  2. Coat each piece with seasoned flour.
  3. Heat oil in a skillet or deep fryer.
  4. Fry the chicken until golden brown and crispy.
  5. Drain on paper towels and serve hot.
